WELCOME TO INTENSA

Information Technology Engineering Student's Association

The Information Technology Engineering Students Association (INTENSA) of Information Technology Department of the Goa College of Engineering was established in the year 2003. INTENSA strives to create a sense of technical, cultural and social vibe amidst the faculty and student and keeps a continuous flow of activities that help them share valuable knowledge and showcase a plethora of talent. The council aims at creating an environment that helps an individual grow technically and socially.

Our Vision

“Impart high quality knowledge & skills to students in the field of Information Technology, motivate research, encourage industry consultancy projects & nurture human values and life skills.”

Our Mission

1

“Impart knowledge and skill based training Information Technology and allied fields.”

2

“Promote research and industrial consultancy projects and establish a linkage with Industry and Alumni.”

3

“Inculcate leadership qualities, human values, concern for environment and society.”

4

“Nurture innovation, entrepreneurship and resource management skills.”

Events

14thAug 2019

"Vrukshabandhan"

INTENSA held a tree plantation drive in collaboration with Pro Active Goans ‘Go Goa Green Initiative’.

11thSep 2019

"Teachers Day"

Team INTENSA celebrated teachers day in IT department, GEC, as opportunity to convey every students wishes to all the teachers, who have given impeccable contribution in shaping us.

3rd- 4thoct 2019

"TechFluence 2k19"

Team INTENSA organised the first edition of their intercollegiate event ‘TechFluence 2k19 - Influenced by Technology’. The two-day programme comprised of various technical events and witnessed the enthusiastic participation of students from colleges across the state.

4thFeb 2020

"Cancer Awarness Fundraiser"

Team INTENSA in the view of “World Cancer Day”, organised a Cancer Awareness fundraiser. The contribution from the program aided cancer patient treatments, through the assistance of Dr. Manoj Prabhudessai, an Indian Medical Association Fel-lowship member.

7thFeb 2020

"Success Without Stress"

Team INTENSA in association with Rotary Club, Ponda organised a session on the topic success without stressby conducted by Dr. Prashant Kakode, a surgeon from Cambridge, and his team who enlighted the audience on various topics related to stress.

20thFeb 2020

"Mental Health Webinar"

Team INTENSA in association with Ms. Tanvi Seth, Counselling Psychologist at Fortis Healthcare Department of Mental Health & Behavioural Sciences, Delhi organised a live webinar whose primary aim was to educate the audience on how to deal with stress and how to maintain their mental health while juggling with hectic daily schedules.

4thMarch 2020

"Yes! I Can Do IT!"

Team INTENSA in association with Mr. Vishwanath Swar, Head of Department of Commerce (HSS), MES College, Vasco organised a Session on Personality Development. The agenda for the talk was how to be positive in life, self-hypnosis with positive affirmations, how to build up good self-esteem, and a few inspiring stories.

7thMarch 2020

"Beyond Engineering"

Team INTENSA in association with GEC-Goa Alumni Asso-ciation, organized a motivational session by Ms. Priya Dalvi, a passionate writer, speaker, blogger and an engineer from our very own Information Technology department of Goa Engineering College. She gave an insight into her book titled “What About PASSION?” which is about the journey of 12 engineers who took up their “sphere of interest” and created stories towards success.

10thOct 2020

"E-Farewell"

INTENSA organised an “E-Farewell 2020” ceremony for the students of ME and BE, the batch of 2020 to mark the end of a long splendid journey of engineering.
